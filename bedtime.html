Bedtime

Although Piper Sue loves to cuddle with all her family and friends, when it's bedtime she loves to cuddle up next to her mumma, Maggie, for a little while before it's time to say goodnight. She will usually crawl down from the bed, and lay on her doggy bed on the floor. She has a high tendency to get lonely, so usually in the middle of the night, she'll jump back in bed and snuggle with her mumma until it's pee time bright and early!
